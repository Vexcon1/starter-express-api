About OSSTF / FEESO

OSSTF is proud to represent over 160 members of the office, clerical, administrative and library support employees at Brock University since July 2002. 


Members of: Anti Bullying Coalition at Brock (ABC) Brock Alliance of Labour Leaders (BALL) Canadian Labour Congress (CLC) Niagara Regional Labour Council (NRLC) Ontario Federation of Labour (OFL) Ontario Secondary Schools Teachers Federation (OSSTF) D. 35 Universities and Colleges.

How It Works

Give to Grow is fundraising platform from Small Scale Farms that helps support local "teams" by providing either $5 cash or a $10 bag of fruits and vegetables in return for every produce bag sold. Buy your produce bag from one of Give to Grow teams today and help them help others!

Sponsor Our Team

Give to Grow is a sustainable, collaborative fundraiser with the potential to benefit our entire community. For every bag of produce purchased from this OSSTF campaign, Small Scale Farms will donate $10 of fresh produce to Community Care St. Catharines/Thorold.
